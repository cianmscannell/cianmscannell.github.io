I am a postdoc at King's College London working on image processing, machine learning and Bayesian inference with cardiovascular MRI data. I have a particular interest in quantitative myocardial perfusion. I am funded by the <a href="https://medicalengineering.org.uk/">Wellcome EPSRC Centre for Medical Engineering</a>.

------
Please get in touch if you are interested in discussing my work, future collaborations or opportunities.
